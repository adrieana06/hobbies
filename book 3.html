IT ENDS WITH US

BY: COLLEEN HOOVER

Lily Bloom’s life has not been easy, yet she works tirelessly to build a better future. Coming from a small Maine town, she graduated from college and established a successful floral business in Boston. Everything seems perfect until she meets Ryle Kincaid, a brilliant neurosurgeon. Their chemistry is undeniable, and Lily feels as if she finally deserves happiness. However, Ryle has a troubling aversion to serious relationships, raising questions about his past and their future.

MY REVIEW

Reading It Ends with Us was an emotional experience for me. I didn’t expect it to be so deep and serious, but it really opened my eyes to what people go through in unhealthy relationships. I liked how the story wasn’t just about romance,it showed the struggle of making the right choice, even when it’s painful. I felt connected to Lily and admired her strength. Some parts were hard to read, but that’s what made it powerful. It made me think a lot about love, pain, and self-worth, and I appreciate how real the story felt
